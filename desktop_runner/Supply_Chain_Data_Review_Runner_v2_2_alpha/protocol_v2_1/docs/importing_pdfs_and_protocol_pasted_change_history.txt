2. Programa Nacional de Fortalecimento da Agricultura Familiar — Pronaf

Pronaf is primarily a public credit and policy program, not a geospatial dataset. The official gov.br service page describes Pronaf as a program to promote sustainable rural development by increasing productive capacity, generating employment, raising income, and improving quality of life for family farmers. It also notes that Pronaf credit is executed by public/private banks and rural credit cooperatives.

However, the same official page says there is a data panel with the historical series of Pronaf credit operations, developed from the Central Bank’s rural credit/proagro system, Sicor, and updated monthly by Brazil’s Ministry of Agrarian Development and Family Farming. That panel is the part that functions like a data source.

Recommendation: split the concept:

Concept	Treatment
Pronaf	program / policy reference
Pronaf credit operations data panel	data source

Ministério do Desenvolvimento Agrário e Agricultura Familiar. (n.d.). Programa Nacional de Fortalecimento da Agricultura Familiar — Pronaf: Painel de dados das operações de crédito. https://www.gov.br/pt-br/servicos/acessar-o-programa-nacional-de-fortalecimento-da-agricultura-familiar-pronaf
